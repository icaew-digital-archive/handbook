# ICAEW Digital Archive Handbook

This handbook provides comprehensive guides, resources, and information about ICAEW’s digital archiving initiatives. Whether you are a user, administrator, or simply seeking relevant resources, you'll find everything you need here.

## User Guides

  These guides are designed to assist you in using ICAEW’s digital archiving platforms:

* [Preservica](./user-guides/preservica/preservica-overview.md) – Our primary digital preservation platform.
* [Web archive collections (including Archive-It)](./user-guides/web-archiving/web-archive-overview.md) – Our web archive collections.

## Admin Guides

These resources are primarily intended for the Digital Archive Manager and Digital Archivist.

  Admin guides mostly focus on the processes involved in:

* The ingestion of materials into our digital preservation platform.
* The capture of web pages using our web crawlers.

## Useful Resources

* A collection of additional resources related to digital preservation and web archiving, here you will find the links to the platforms.
